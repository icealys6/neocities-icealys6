BITTER TEARS

In this novel the author strays from historical fiction to the romance genre with a dash of suspense and drama. This is a novel about fatal attraction and obsession, greed and jealousy and what it can do to a happy marriage and lead to far-reaching consequences. Meet the main characters, Elizabeth Connors, and her handsome and charismatic husband who is a prominent cardiologist. Dr. Thomas Brooks is the darling of the high society bluebloods who live in the wealthy suburbs on the north shore of Chicago. His sociopathic daughter-in-law, Hillary, hatches a devilish plan that will shatter many lives and wreak havoc on the entire Brooks' family. The novel shows how people must reassess their lives in the midst of a crisis and choose another pathway. Read to see how Hillary's poisonous fatal attraction for Tom evolves into a terrifying situation that nearly destroys the marriage and even places Elizabeth's life in jeopardy.
