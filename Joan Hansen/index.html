About Joan Hansen

100 YEARS IN MIAMI-BETRAYAL UNDER THE PALMS

Joan's first novel
Available now at online book outlets in both regular and e-book format.

Joan's first historical fiction novel is based on over 100 years in the fascinating city of Miami. The novel encompasses the years 1896-2000. The family saga starts in 1896 when the dashing Donegon brothers take the straggly village by storm and help clear the land for Henry Flagler's railroad and Miami's first major hotel, and sweeps all the way to millennium. As Miami grows, so does the family fortune as the Donegon hotel dynasty spreads across the world, but there is a veil of secrecy that hides many sins through the generations. Katlin, fourth generation Donegon, and modern-day knockout and heiress, seems to have it all - beauty, wealth and power. But when tragedy strikes, she learns the shocking family secret that will change her life and set her on a new path of discovery. Set in sizzling Miami, the trendy city rocking to an art Deco beat and hiding many secrets behind the turquoise and flamingo facades -step behind the glittering surfaces to discover the startling BETRAYAL UNDER THE PALMS.

COMING ATTRACTIONS:

LITTLE SAIGON ON THE STREET OF DREAMS FULFILLED

Joan has a new novel currently in the works that follows the fate of the members of a Vietnamese family after the fall of Saigon in 1975 at the end of the Vietnam War. The family gets separated from each other during the rush to get out of the war-torn city now under the rule of the Communists. They encounter many trials and tribulations, suffering and joy, as they go off in many different directions and lose touch with each other. They each dream of someday reuniting as a family in the United States.
